Mediscape

  • High Concept: Find the exit to save your life.
  • Player Experience Goals: Excitement and minor frustration.
  • Genre: Action/Platformer
  • Game play: The player jumps from platform to platform, escaping death by dodging enemies that want you silenced.
  • Prototype goal: To gain a better understanding of game states and Arcade physics.
  • Features: Adorable kittens, scary ghosts, towering heights, and mucho action.
  • Setting/Premise: The world is bleak. Resources are running out, so man has turned to completely relying on animal testing.
  • Story: The game centers around animal experiment case number 592EB46-7, a kitten who was tested for cognitive ability, attempting to aid in hyper intelligent humans. However, the kitten was more than smart--he became self-aware. He now seeks to escape to the door in sight, hoping for a free life with his inexplicable ninja skills.
  • Target Audience: All ages, but targeting children 18 and younger.
  • Play time: 2-3 minutes.
  • Strategies: Always move left!
  • Prototype Assets: Various assets from Open Game Art and 1 picture (start screen) from Google images. Author and date noted in the photo.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Doodle jump?
